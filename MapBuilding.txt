All Tilemaps on which players walks need to have "Platform" Tag and "ground" Layer (Interactive as well).
As well as RigidBody and Tilemap collider

Spikes need to have "spikes" Layer.


And you need to add UI "Tooltip text" to the Chests, Doors, Levers etc.

Also you need to add Tilemap to lever.

Sound and Scene controllers must be in all of the scenes.

Pause controller must be in all level scenes.
